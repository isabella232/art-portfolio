Celestial Wheel on Webtoon
Webcomic synopsis: For Bing’s whole life she’s only wanted to be someone everyone in the Celestial Court knew and respected. She hoped her first New Moon festival would be a chance to finally fit in. For a gleaming moment it seems like that may happen when an oracle magically marks her as the hero who will save the twelve Celestial clans from a faceless beast. But a cruel twist of fate causes Bing to keep her mark a secret and if she’s not careful everyone may end up hunting her.
